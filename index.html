Dental Crowns

Dental crowns are custom-made caps placed over damaged or weakened teeth to restore their strength, shape, and appearance. At CitiDental, we use high-quality porcelain and ceramic crowns that match the natural color of your teeth for a seamless look. Crowns are recommended for teeth that are severely decayed, cracked, broken, or have undergone root canal treatment. Our restorative dentistry team ensures a precise fit for maximum comfort and durability.
